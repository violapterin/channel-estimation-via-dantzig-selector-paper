Let \m {\hat {\V {g}}} be the Dantzig Selector.
Split \m {\V{g}} into two parts: \m {\V {g} _\MC {A}}, the largest \m {S} positions of \m {\V{g}}, \m {\V {g} _\MC {A}} the next \m {S} largest positions of \m {\V{g}}, and \m {\V {g} _\MC {K}} are the positions complement to \m {\V {g} _\MC {A}}.

{

\Disp {
\NC \V{v} \DB {\s \SB {n}}

\NC n =\NC 1, \ldots, N_p-1 \NR

For fixed \m {S}, define
\Disp {
